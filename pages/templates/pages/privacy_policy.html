Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are very important to us at EduVoteGH.

Information We Collect

EduVoteGH collects limited voter identification information such as names, student IDs, or institutional credentials depending on the requirements of the election being conducted. This information is used strictly for voter verification purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for conducting secure digital elections. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

Data Protection

EduVoteGH implements strong security measures including access controls, encryption, and secure server infrastructure to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Data Retention

Election data may be stored temporarily to allow election verification, auditing, and result validation. After the required retention period, sensitive voter information may be archived or securely deleted.

User Rights

Users have the right to request clarification on how their information is used within the platform. Educational institutions administering the election may also manage voter data in accordance with their internal policies.

Policy Updates

EduVoteGH reserves the right to update this privacy policy when necessary to improve transparency, security, and compliance with data protection standards.
